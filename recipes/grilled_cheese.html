Grilled Cheese

Here is the recipe for Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2-3 slices cheese (cheddar, American, or Swiss)
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened

Instructions

  • Butter one side of each bread slice completely to edges
  • Place one slice butter-side down in a cold pan
  • Top with cheese slices
  • Place second bread slice butter-side up on top of cheese
  • Heat pan over medium-low heat
  • Cook for 3-4 minutes until bottom is golden brown
  • Flip carefully with spatula
  • Cook another 2-3 minutes until second side is golden and cheese melts
  • Remove from heat and let cool 1 minute before cutting
