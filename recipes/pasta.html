Pasta with Parmesan and Butter

Here is the recipe for Pasta

Ingredients

  • Raw Pasta from store
  • Water
  • Butter
  • Parmesan Cheese

Instructions

  • Add water to pot
  • Bring water to a boil
  • Add pasta to boiling water
  • Cook pasta according to package directions
  • Drain water from pot
  • Toss with butter and grated Parmesan cheese
